Welcome to the  This Mode ! This game helps you practice adding numbers with clear spoken questions.

Objective of the Game
You will hear or see a story problem . Your task is to find the total sum of two numbers and enter the correct answer.

Gameplay Instructions

Listen carefully to the story question, for example: “Sam has {a} pencils and finds {b} more. How many pencils does he have now?”

Enter your answer in the input field.

Press the Submit button to check your answer.

Tips

Take your time to understand the story before answering.

Use the Read Question button to hear the question again.

Accessibility Features

Text-to-Speech will clearly speak the question.

Buttons are labeled for screen readers."
